3) Latest News Manager

Create and manage each news item. Required fields include slug, date, and at least one language for title and overview/body content. Optional visual fields include card photo, hero photo, and gallery photos.

You can publish in English only, Traditional Chinese only, or both. Hero Photo controls the large horizontal image behind the title on detail page. If card/hero is empty, website will use the AAIA branded placeholder image automatically.
